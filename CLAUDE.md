# Claude Code Instructions

## Formatting

### Em dashes
Always format em dashes with a non-breaking space before the dash and no space after, like this: `⁠—`. Do not use ` — ` (spaces on both sides) or `—` (no spaces).

### Quotation marks and apostrophes
Always use curly (typographic) quotes and apostrophes, not straight quotes. For contractions and possessives, use the right single quotation mark: `'` (U+2019), not the straight apostrophe `'` (U+0027).
